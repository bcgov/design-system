## Accessibility

The [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG20/)Â are the internationally recognized guidelines for web accessibility. The Province is committed to meeting WCAG 2.0 and 2.1 Level AA. All design system components meet or exceed these standards.
